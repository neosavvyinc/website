Working With Neosavvy

We’re proud of the work we do at Neosavvy. It’s dynamic, engaging and constantly challenges us to learn more, do more, and share more. We’re looking for talented developers who share our passion for both software development and advancing a nimble, engaged, and collaborative consulting team. (And the benefits are awesome, too.)

We’re looking for developers with technical aptitude and the people skills to match. It’s important that everyone on our team takes an iterative, client-centric approach to projects. We also want team members to have effective communication skills, be adaptable and flexible, and constantly strive on-time deployment.

We expect developers to demonstrate a solid understanding of full stack development in HTML/CSS/Javascript hooked into a Scala-, Java-, or Rails-based JSON/REST-ful API. We currently focus on Javascript frontend development, but also take on projects with Scala-, Java-, or Python-based backends exposed as REST-ful services.

Benefits include:
  • Competitive salary
  • Goal to install 12.5% of your salary in an SEP-IRA
  • A Mac, mobile device, MiFi, and at-home Internet—all paid by Neosavvy
  • Paid developer tools and training
  • Participation in company-sponsored events (dinners, concerts, etc.)
  • Full membership to Equinox Gyms
  • Free lunch for everyone who helps our team advance

If you’re interested in growing your career with Neosavvy—and helping Neosavvy grow—send us a note and tell us about yourself. Include a link to your LinkedIn and Github profiles, past project experience, and any other spare-time development projects (with Git-SVN commit history) so we can see how you can help us and how we can help you.

Let’s work together.
